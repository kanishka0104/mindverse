## First Time Setup

### Step 1: Add Your First Task
1. Click the **"Add Task"** button on the dashboard
2. Fill in:
   - **Subject**: e.g., "Mathematics"
   - **Description**: e.g., "Complete Chapter 5 exercises"
   - **Estimated Time**: 60 minutes
   - **Priority**: High
   - **Date**: Today's date (pre-filled)
3. Click **"Save Task"**

### Step 2: Start a Study Session
1. Go to **Focus Timer** page (use navigation)
2. Select your task from the dropdown
3. Click **"Start"** button
4. Study with focus!
5. Take breaks when prompted
6. Click **"Save Progress"** when done

### Step 3: Set Weekly Goal
1. Navigate to **Goals** page
2. Click **"Set New Goal"**
3. Enter:
   - **Goal Title**: e.g., "Complete 10 hours of study"
   - **Target Time**: 600 minutes (10 hours)
4. Click **"Save Goal"**
5. Track your progress throughout the week

### Step 4: Daily Reflection
1. At the end of your study day, visit **Reflection** page
2. Select your mood emoji
3. Fill in reflection questions:
   - What went well
   - Challenges faced
   - Key learnings
   - Tomorrow's plan
4. Rate your focus and energy levels
5. Click **"Save Reflection"**

## Common Questions

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device.

**Q: Will I lose data if I close the browser?**
A: No! Data persists even after closing browser, restarting computer, etc.

**Q: Can I use this on multiple devices?**
A: Each device has its own data. Use Export feature to transfer data between devices.

**Q: How do I backup my data?**
A: Go to Export page and download JSON backup regularly (monthly recommended).

**Q: What if I refresh during a timer session?**
A: Timer state is saved! It will restore when you return to the page.

**Q: Can I use this offline?**
A: Yes! Works completely offline after initial load.

**Q: How do I change the theme?**

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: App not loading**
- Solution: Ensure all files are in same folder
- Check that JavaScript is enabled in browser
- Try a different browser

**Problem: Data not saving**
- Solution: Check that cookies/localStorage is enabled
- Ensure not in private/incognito mode
- Check browser storage isn't full

**Problem: Timer not working**
- Solution: Refresh the page
- Ensure you selected a task first
- Check browser console for errors (F12)

**Problem: Theme not changing**
- Solution: Clear browser cache
- Check localStorage is enabled
- Try manually refreshing (Ctrl+F5)
